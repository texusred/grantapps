Application Guidelines

Before submitting your grant application, please review the following guidelines:

  • All applications must be submitted at least 4 weeks before funds are required
  • Applications must include justification for all requested items
  • Where possible, include links to sources for cost estimates
  • Equipment purchases must be necessary for the club/society's core activities
  • The Students' Union reserves the right to request additional information
  • Incomplete applications will be returned without consideration
Frequently Asked Questions
How much funding can my club/society apply for?

There is no set limit, but funding is allocated based on need, justification, and available resources. Larger requests may require more detailed justification.

When will I know if my application is approved?

Applications are typically reviewed within 2 weeks of the application period closing. You will be notified by email once a decision has been made.

Can I apply for funding for past events or purchases?

No, funding cannot be approved retrospectively. All applications must be submitted and approved before any purchases are made.

What supporting documents should I include?

Include quotes for equipment, venue hire agreements, event proposals, and any other relevant documentation that supports your application. These should be attached to your email.

Can we charge admission for funded events?

Yes, but any projected income should be noted in your application. Funding may be adjusted based on expected income. You can use our Events Budget Planner to help you plan your events.

What happens if we don't use all the allocated funds?

Unused funds are reclaimed by the Students' Union in December, April and July.
